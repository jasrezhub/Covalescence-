Post-session care

Aftercare guide

Your tattoo is a wound and a work of art. How you care for it in the coming weeks determines how it heals — and how it looks for the rest of your life.

First 24 hours

Immediately after your session

  • Leave the wrap your artist applied on for 2–4 hours (or overnight if using second-skin film).
  • Gently wash with lukewarm water and unscented soap using clean hands.
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel — do not rub.
  • Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser or tattoo balm.
  • Wear loose, clean clothing over the area.

Days 2–7

The healing stage

  • Wash gently 2× daily and moisturise after each wash.
  • Do not pick, scratch, or peel any flaking skin.
  • Avoid submerging in water (baths, pools, ocean). Showers are fine.
  • Keep the tattoo out of direct sunlight.

Weeks 2–4

The long heal

  • Continue daily moisturising until skin is back to normal texture.
  • Begin applying SPF 50 whenever the tattoo is exposed to sunlight.

Long-term

Keeping it sharp for years

  • SPF every time you're outdoors — UV is the fastest way to fade fine line work.
  • Keep skin well-moisturised year-round.
  • Touch-ups recommended for fine line work after 1–2 years.

Notice something wrong?

Signs of infection include excessive redness, swelling, or fever. Contact a medical professional promptly. Reach Ember at hello@inkandember.com.
